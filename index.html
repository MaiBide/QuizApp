Background

Here are some health concerns about routine medical checkups:

  • The lower limit of the Vitamin D range (20-30ng/mL 25-hydroxy D) on routine blood tests panels most probably does not promote good health
  • Tests for levels of Hc, Lp(a), and C-RP are generally missing
  • The upper limit of Fasting insulin (~15uU/ml), which is generally considered safe could be too high.
  • If Fasting glucose level is normal (<=99mg/dl), the Fasting insulin level and glucose-to-insulin ratio aren't normally checked.
  • The more recently proposed HbA1c level (<=5.5%) for good health is not generally checked or used.
